Square Cursor - Fit to Border

Hover over elements to see the cursor expand and fit their borders. The cursor dynamically resizes to match element dimensions.

Dynamic Border Fit

When you hover over elements, the square cursor expands to fit their borders. This creates a unique interactive experience.

How It Works

JavaScript calculates the position and dimensions of hovered elements, then animates the cursor to match their borders.
